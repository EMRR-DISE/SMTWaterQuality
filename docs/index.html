However, secchi depth is subject to some degree of variability with observer, weather, time of day, waves, and whether the observer has had their coffee. Turbidity, as measured by a sonde, is also subject to some variability depending on when the sonde was last calibrated. To convert between turbidity and secchi depth for the purpose of this document, we used secchi depth data from an integrated dataset of discrete water quality (Bashevkin et al, 2013), collected by IEP surveys from 2010-2022 for the South Delta (area shown in map above). The secchi depths were matched to the nearest continuous station's daily average turbidity.
